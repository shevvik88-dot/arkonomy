Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 20, 2026

Arkonomy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with when you use the Arkonomy app.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide you with personalised financial insights:

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account.
  • Bank transactions — connected via Plaid. We receive read-only access to transaction history, account balances, and institution names. We do not store your bank credentials.
  • Financial goals and savings targets — entered manually by you within the app.
  • Investment activity — orders placed through your connected Alpaca brokerage account (amount, symbol, status).
  • Usage data — feature interactions, error logs, and session metadata used to improve app reliability and performance. This data does not include the content of your financial transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to power Arkonomy's features:

  • Generating AI-driven financial insights and spending summaries.
  • Calculating savings progress and round-up amounts.
  • Executing investment orders on your behalf via Alpaca.
  • Personalising recommendations based on your spending patterns.
  • Diagnosing errors and improving app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your personal financial management.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure, hosted on AWS. We apply the following controls:

  • Encryption in transit — all data is transmitted over TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at rest — all stored data is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Row-level security (RLS) — enforced at the database layer so each user can only access their own records, even in the event of an application-layer misconfiguration.
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) — required for all production system access by Arkonomy personnel.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Arkonomy integrates with the following third parties to deliver its core functionality:

Plaid

Used to securely connect your bank account and retrieve transaction data. Plaid's own privacy policy governs how it handles your financial institution credentials. plaid.com/legal/privacy-notice

Alpaca Markets

Used to execute fractional investment orders (e.g. buying SPY). Alpaca is a separate, independently licensed broker-dealer. Your brokerage account is held with Alpaca, not Arkonomy. alpaca.markets/disclosures

Anthropic (Claude AI)

Used to generate financial insights from your anonymised spending summaries. We send aggregated financial data (not raw transactions) to Anthropic's API. anthropic.com/privacy

Stripe

Used to process subscription payments for Arkonomy Pro. Stripe handles all payment card data directly — Arkonomy never stores or processes your card details. stripe.com/privacy

Vercel

Used to host and serve the Arkonomy web application. Vercel may process request metadata (IP address, user-agent) in the course of delivering the service. vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with any third party beyond those listed above and only to the extent necessary to provide the service.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding the personal data we hold about you:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion — request that your data be deleted.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact Viktor Sheviakov at privacy@arkonomy.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.
